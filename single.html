The best cardio exercises you can do at home

The best cardio exercises you can do at home Cardio exercises strengthen the heart, blood vessels and lungs, increase endurance, improve metabolism and make the body slim and beautiful.Performing cardio exercises is much more affordable than you are you think. You don't have to go to the gym to get high-quality and effective cardio training. It can be done at home, in the yard, in the Park, and even on the street. Here are some cardio exercises that are suitable for this.Walking or running on the the stairs.Go up and down the stairs several times.It works the muscles through the entire range. Increase the frequency of repetitions.Set goals for yourself.1. Walk to the stairs 5 times.2. to the stairs 10 times.3. to the stairs 15 times.4. to the stairs 20 times.5. to the stairs 30 times.6. Run on the stairs 5 times.What cardio exercises are you able to do at home?Drag your heels.Make a slow circular motion with your heels.Stretching the heels is a great way to stretch the heels.
